SAMPLE TOUR

7 Days Sabah & Sarawak
Photography Tour

A sample itinerary designed for bird photography, rainforest trails, rare species, and local homestay experiences.

RM11,900

Per person · Full board package

Day 1

Hotel / Bakelalan

Morning pick up from hotel and depart to Bakelalan. Arrive late afternoon. Night birding for frogmouth, owl, and more.

Overnight at local homestay · LD

Day 2

Bakelalan

Daytime birding and photography. Night birding session for owls and nocturnal species.

Overnight at local homestay · BLD

Day 3

Bakelalan / Paya Maga

Drive to Paya Maga and trek up the trail, arriving late afternoon. Evening spotting for frogmouth, owl, and other wildlife.

Overnight at local homestay · BLD

Day 4

Paya Maga / Klias

Morning birding for the rare and endemic Black Oriole. Afternoon trek down to the car, then drive to Klias for the Hook-billed Bulbul.

Overnight at resthouse · BLD

Day 5

Klias / Tambunan

Drive to Tambunan and visit Gunung Alab for a mix of lowland and montane birds before checking in to the lodge.

Overnight at local lodge · BLD

Day 6

Tambunan

Birding along Trusmadi Road with chances to photograph Bulwer’s Partridge, Bornean Banded Pitta, Crimson-headed Partridge, Red-breasted Partridge, and more.

Overnight at local lodge · BLD

Day 7

Tambunan / Airport

Depart to Kota Kinabalu International Airport after the final morning of the tour.

Departure day · BL
